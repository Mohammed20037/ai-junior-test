## Notes
- All prices are in USD and include applicable taxes.
- The chicken served at NovaBite is grilled, not fried (except for Chicken Tenders on the Kids Menu).
- NovaBite does NOT have vegan pasta labeled as "vegan pasta." However, the Penne Arrabbiata and Thai Green Curry are fully vegan main courses. The Vegan Pad Thai is also available.
- Gluten-free pasta substitution is available for any pasta dish for an additional $2.00.
- NovaBite does NOT serve sushi, tacos, or pizza (except Kids Mini Pizza).
