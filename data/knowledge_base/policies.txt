# NovaBite Restaurants – Branch Information & Policies

## Branch Locations

### Downtown Branch
- Address: 120 Main Street, Downtown, New York, NY 10001
- Phone: (212) 555-0101
- Seating Capacity: 80 guests
- Parking: Street parking only; validated parking at Central Garage ($5 flat fee with receipt)
- Special Features: Rooftop terrace (seasonal, May–September), live jazz on Friday evenings

### Midtown Branch
- Address: 450 Lexington Avenue, Midtown, New York, NY 10017
- Phone: (212) 555-0202
- Seating Capacity: 120 guests
- Parking: Basement valet parking available ($12)
- Special Features: Private dining room for up to 20 guests, full bar

### Brooklyn Heights Branch
- Address: 78 Atlantic Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11201
- Phone: (718) 555-0303
- Seating Capacity: 60 guests
- Parking: Dedicated lot behind the restaurant (free)
- Special Features: Dog-friendly outdoor patio, weekend brunch service

## Opening Hours

### Weekday Hours (Monday – Thursday)
- All Branches: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- Kitchen closes at 9:30 PM

### Friday Hours
- All Branches: 11:00 AM – 11:30 PM
- Kitchen closes at 11:00 PM

### Weekend Hours (Saturday & Sunday)
- Downtown & Midtown: 10:00 AM – 11:30 PM
- Brooklyn Heights: 9:00 AM – 11:00 PM (brunch served 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM)
- Kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time at all branches

### Holiday Hours
- NovaBite is CLOSED on Christmas Day (December 25) and New Year's Day (January 1).
- Reduced hours on Thanksgiving: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM (all branches).
- All other public holidays: regular hours apply.

## Reservation Policy
- Reservations are recommended for parties of 4 or more.
- Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability.
- Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance.
- A credit card is required for parties of 8 or more to hold the reservation.
- Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the reserved time to avoid a $25 per-person no-show fee.
- Maximum party size for standard reservation: 20 guests. Larger groups should contact the Events team.

## Refund Policy
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
- If a meal is unsatisfactory, guests should notify the manager on duty immediately.
- Takeout orders may be refunded if reported within 30 minutes of pickup.
- Delivery order complaints must be filed within 1 hour of delivery.
- Gift cards are non-refundable but never expire.

## Loyalty Program – NovaBite Rewards
- Free to join. Sign up in-store or through the NovaBite app.
- Earn 1 point per $1 spent on food and non-alcoholic beverages.
- Alcoholic beverages do NOT earn loyalty points.
- Reward Tiers:
  - Bronze (0–199 points): Birthday dessert on us
  - Silver (200–499 points): 10% off every visit + birthday dessert
  - Gold (500+ points): 15% off every visit + birthday dessert + priority reservations + free delivery
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
- Points cannot be transferred between accounts.
- Rewards cannot be combined with other promotional offers.

## Event Hosting & Catering

### Private Events
- Available at all branches for parties of 10–80 guests.
- Requires booking at least 7 days in advance.
- A dedicated event coordinator will be assigned.
- Custom menus available; pricing starts at $45 per person.

### Birthday Package
- Price: $35 per person (minimum 10 guests)
- Includes: 3-course set menu, a birthday cake, decorations, and a complimentary drink per guest.
- Booking: Must be booked at least 5 days in advance via phone or email.
- Branches: Available at all locations.

### Premium Catering Package
- Price: $65 per person (minimum 20 guests)
- Includes: Chef's tasting menu (5 courses), premium wine pairing (2 glasses per guest), dedicated wait staff, custom table setup and floral arrangement, complimentary dessert bar.
- Booking: Must be booked at least 14 days in advance.
- Branches: Available at Midtown and Downtown branches only.

### Corporate Event Package
- Price: $55 per person (minimum 15 guests)
- Includes: 4-course meal, AV equipment (projector, screen, microphone), dedicated meeting space for up to 4 hours, coffee and tea service throughout.
- Branches: Available at Midtown branch only (private dining room).

## Dietary Accommodations
- NovaBite can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free diets.
- Please inform your server of any allergies BEFORE ordering.
- While we take precautions, our kitchen handles all major allergens and we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
- Halal and kosher options are NOT currently available.

## Dress Code
- Smart casual at all branches.
- No beachwear, flip-flops, or athletic wear during dinner service (after 5:00 PM).
- The rooftop terrace at Downtown branch is more relaxed during lunch hours.
